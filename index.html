No Hells, No Heavens

No Hells, No Heavens is an in-development browser-based cooperative action-RPG, using Hercules and roBrowser as a base for the server and client.

Pick one of six distinct character classes and fight your way through mobs of ferocious creatures and vile demons, find some cool loot after the battle, re-arm yourself with more powerful equipment, and then do it again!

As your character grows stronger, you can choose to hone your skills further, or branch out and pick a second (and eventually a third!) class to gain access to new abilities. A melee mage-knight? An archer who wields healing magic? A guy that can just tank damage really well? The possibilities are literally limitless 216!

NHNH is a passion project of mine that has undergone more than a couple complete system redesigns in the past however many months I’ve been working on it. I’ve finally settled on a design that I really like though, so expect a public playable build sooner rather than later!

QuickRPG

QuickRPG gives you all of the fun and excitement of traditional RPGs, but without all the wasted time that comes with things like “character creation” and “gameplay.”

Simply send a tweet to the QuickRPG Bot, then sit back and enjoy the story of your automatically generated character as they seek to avenge their automatically generated family.

Will they succeed in their quest? Will they fall in battle to a common Slime? Find out the answers instantly, then be free to return to your busy life.

PICO-8 Minigames

PICO-8 is a full-fledged game development environment using Lua for an imaginary game console, aptly named the PICO-8. It imposes very harsh memory, storage, and graphical limits on the developer; these constraints create a very challenging and interesting platform to work with.

Here’s a few of the PICO-8 games I’ve made:

BONES is a fast-paced top-down arcade shooter. Take hold of your trusty longbow and see how long you can survive against waves of the undead!

KIWI HD is a reimagining of the first game I ever made.

Kiwi Classic was a high-octane platformer, requiring intense concentration and quick reflexes; KIWI HD brings back the gameplay that tens of fans around the world fell in love with, featuring a beautiful soundtrack, and all-new stunning 128x128 16-color graphics.

TEXT FIGHTER 2 TURBO is an experiment to see how the fighting game genre would play out if it was turn-based instead of real-time.

Punch! Kick! Do a special move! Utilize "yomi," the Japanese technique of reading the mind of the opponent, to out-wit and out-skill your barebones AI enemy!

radial

radial is a simple top-down arcade shooter, made for a game jam. Protect your planet against incoming waves of alien enemies and aim for the high score!

radial was my first attempt at using Unity, and I feel like I really learned a lot from it. For example, I learned how quaternions can be used to represent the rotation of objects in a 3D space, and I learned that I really don’t like working with Unity. :^)

Gone to the Dogs

Gone to the Dogs (working title!) is an action platformer with an emphasis on exploration, inspired by the Metroid and Momodora series of games.

Four elemental beasts, once sealed away by an angelic warrior, have returned to the world; guide The Heroine through the caverns of Mt. Emanon, discover new powers and abilities, and slay the newly-awoken demons before they regain their full strength!

Can Love Bloom on the Battlefield?

Riley is sleeving up their deck for a big upcoming tournament; play through a branching story and guide Riley on the path to glorious victory, crushing defeat, or possibly even… love?
